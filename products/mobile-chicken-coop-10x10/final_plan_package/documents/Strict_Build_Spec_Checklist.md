# Strict Build-Spec Checklist

Use this checklist as the master source of truth for the PDF, CAD drawings, Etsy images, and pre-publish approval gate.

| System | Build-spec item | Required value | Acceptance check | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dimensions | Outside base footprint | 120 in x 120 in | Diagonal measurements match within 1/4 in before framing upward | Required |
| Dimensions | Lower run height | 48 in target mobile height | All lower run posts and side elevation use 48 in; no 54 in/72 in mixed into this product | Required |
| Dimensions | Elevated coop footprint | 48 in x 120 in | Coop floor lands over rear 48 in of run; floor is square and supported | Required |
| Dimensions | Upper coop wall heights | 48 in high side / 40 in low side | Roof has approx. 8 in fall across 48 in depth | Required |
| Frame | Skids | Two 4x6 PT skids, 120 in | Skids are parallel, bevelled at tow/front end, and through-fastened to base as needed | Required |
| Frame | Base rails | 2x6 PT perimeter and cross ties | Base is square; cross ties installed near 48, 72, and 96 in from front | Required |
| Frame | 4x4 post count | Nine 4x4 posts | Three post lines: front, coop-front, rear. Prior 6-post draft is superseded | Corrected |
| Frame | Diagonal bracing | 2x4 braces at sides, rear, and coop support line | Frame cannot rack when pushed before wire/siding is installed | Required |
| Coop floor | Floor joists | 2x6 joists about 16 in O.C. | No unsupported plywood span greater than practical joist spacing | Required |
| Coop floor | Floor decking | 3/4 in exterior plywood | Edges supported, seams fastened, edges sealed | Required |
| Walls | Siding/sheathing | 1/2 in exterior plywood or T1-11 | All cut edges primed/sealed; no predator-access gaps | Required |
| Roof | Rafters | 2x4 rafters, approx. 60 in | Overhang included; rafters tied or screwed to wall plates | Required |
| Roof | Roof material | Lightweight corrugated panels | Panels overlap per manufacturer; roofing screws use washers | Required |
| Wire | Hardware cloth | 1/2 in 19-ga galvanized | Attached with screws plus fender washers or battens every 6-8 in | Required |
| Wire | Predator apron | 12-24 in fold-out optional but recommended | Apron staked or clipped where predator pressure exists | Recommended |
| Mobility | Wheel size/rating | 20 in pneumatic or trailer-rated wheels, 600 lb+ each | Wheel rating exceeds finished load with margin; test roll before birds are inside | Required |
| Mobility | Axle | 3/4 in axle rod or hub/spindle assemblies | Retained with collars/cotter pins; mounts through-bolted | Required |
| Mobility | Tow tongue | 4x4 wood or steel tongue with tow ring/chain | Pull point aligned with skids; safety chain installed | Required |
| Doors | Lower access door | Approx. 30 in x 42 in | Door swings/latches cleanly; two-step predator latch | Required |
| Doors | Rear cleanout doors | Two large rear doors | Allow rake/scrape access to full coop floor | Required |
| Doors | Pop door | Approx. 12 in x 14 in | Locks shut at night; ramp hinge or cleats secure | Required |
| Interior | Nest boxes | Three boxes about 12 in W x 12 in H x 14 in D | External/rear access hatch; boxes lower than roost bars | Layer mode |
| Interior | Roost bars | 10-12 linear ft total, removable | Bars removable for broiler mode and cleaning | Layer mode |
| Finish | Surface protection | Exterior paint/stain and sealed plywood edges | No raw plywood edges exposed to weather | Required |
| Safety | Publication claim | DIY plans only | No engineered/code-approved/field-tested claims unless documented | Blocker |
