A: Yes. Broiler mode uses the lower run as the primary space and removes or ignores nest boxes and roosts. Broilers should have ground-level access, clean bedding/grass, shade, water, feed, and frequent moves; verify your animal-care requirements locally.

**Q: Do I need a permit?**
A: Permitting depends on your jurisdiction. Check with your local building department before building.

**Q: What predators does it resist?**
A: The plan uses 1/2" galvanized hardware cloth, screws with fender washers or batten strips, latch guidance, and optional hardware-cloth apron notes. No coop is fully predator-proof; verify your local pressure (raccoons, foxes, dogs, hawks, snakes, rats, etc.) and adjust. Do not use chicken wire for predator protection.
