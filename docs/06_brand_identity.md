# 06 — Brand Identity Package

## Voice
- Plain-spoken, practical, builder-to-builder.
- No hype, no superlatives ("ultimate", "world's best").
- Honest about what plans do and don't include.
- Never makes engineering, code, animal-medical, or legal claims.
- Use farm-build manual language: "DIY digital plans", "pasture rotation",
  "materials list", "build sequence", and "verify locally before building".

## Brand
- **Shop name:** PastureFrame Plans
- **Product line:** Mobile Coop Build Plans
- **Tagline:** DIY pasture coop plans for practical homesteads.
- **Designer credit:** Designed by Ashton Aschenbrener

Use the designer credit on the shop page, PDF cover, and Etsy listing. Do not
say "engineered", "code-approved", "licensed builder", "field-tested", or
"built" unless those claims are true and documented.

## Positioning
PastureFrame Plans sells practical digital plans for mobile coops and homestead
structures. The plans are designed for DIY builders who want clear dimensions,
build steps, material lists, and pasture-ready layouts.

## Color Palette
- Deep Pasture Green `#1F3B24` — primary
- Warm Lumber Tan `#C89B61` — wood tone
- Cream Paper `#F7F3E8` — background
- Charcoal `#252525` — text
- Rust Brown `#8A4B2F` — accent
- Hardware-Cloth Gray `#7D8178` — technical lines

## Typography
- Headings: Inter Bold (or Nunito Bold).
- Body: Source Serif (or Lora) for readability in PDF plans.
- Captions: Inter Regular.

## Logo Brief (deliverable to designer or Ashton in Figma)
- Wordmark: PastureFrame Plans in a clean, readable sans-serif.
- Square icon: simple line-art mobile chicken coop silhouette on skids/wheels,
  one chicken icon, and a small pasture line underneath.
- Keep the mark simple enough to work as an Etsy shop icon.
- File outputs: SVG, PNG @ 1024 / 512 / 256 / 64.
