# Confirmed Design Decisions

| Decision | Final value | Reason / note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Footprint | 10 ft x 10 ft outside base | Matches original project requirement and current plan set. |
| Upper coop | 4 ft x 10 ft elevated house over rear 48 in | Keeps full lower run footprint while providing enclosed layer housing. |
| Lower run height | 48 in | Set as the mobile version height. A 54 in or 72 in variant should be a heavier-duty redesign. |
| Mobility | 4x6 skids + axle wheels + tow tongue | Confirmed final system. Do not use small swivel casters on pasture. |
| Wheel position | Near heavier rear/coop end | Reduces tongue lift load and helps movement over uneven pasture. |
| Hardware cloth | 1/2 in galvanized hardware cloth, 19 ga minimum | Use screws with fender washers or battens. Chicken wire is not predator protection. |
| Layer capacity | 10-12 laying hens | Conservative capacity for 40 sq ft upper coop with nests and roosts. |
| Broiler capacity | 30-45 broilers | Conservative pasture-rotation range; not a fixed guarantee. |
| Prototype status | CAD/plans-first until prototype exists | Do not claim field-tested, built, or proven until real prototype photos exist. |
| Publishing status | Blocked until proof assets are complete | Listing can be drafted, but not approved for launch until blockers are cleared. |
