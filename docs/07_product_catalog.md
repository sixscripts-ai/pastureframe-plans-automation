## Product 1 — Sellable Claim
Digital DIY plans for a 10' x 10' mobile chicken coop/run with a 4' x 10'
elevated coop house. Designed for approximately 10-12 laying hens or 30-45
broilers, depending on setup, climate, management, and movement frequency.

## Product 1 — Required Corrections Before Publishing
- Primary version uses a 48"-54" lower run, not a 6' walk-in run.
- Heavy-duty walk-in variant is optional and must be labeled as ATV/UTV/tractor-towed.
- Mobility uses a skid-and-wheel system, not four small swivel casters.
- Main predator mesh is 1/2" galvanized hardware cloth, 19-gauge minimum.
- Add diagonal bracing, plywood shear strength, exact doors, exact roof slope,
  pop-door/ramp dimensions, hardware-cloth attachment detail, and estimated weight.

## Product 1 — Proof Assets Before Listing
- Hand sketch showing 10' x 10' base and 4' x 10' elevated coop.
- CAD/SketchUp top view, side view, and exploded frame screenshot.
- Close-up wheel/skid system drawing.
- Dimensioned nesting-box and roost layout.
- Material-stack photo if building a prototype.
- Three in-progress build photos if prototype starts.
- Finished prototype photo before claiming "built" or "field-tested".
- Listing image that says "DIGITAL PLANS — NO PHYSICAL COOP SHIPPED".
