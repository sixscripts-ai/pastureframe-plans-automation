# Drawing Requirements for Missing Diagrams

Each drawing should be original to Ashton/PastureFrame and should match the build-spec checklist exactly. Do not publish listing images that imply proof of a built prototype unless real prototype photos exist.

| Diagram | Purpose | Required labels | Approval criteria |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Top view | Shows 10 x 10 footprint, 4 x 10 coop zone, post lines, skids, tow tongue, wheel/axle line | 120 in width, 120 in length, 48 in coop depth, 72 in open front run, 9 posts, axle line, tow/front end | Scale clear; all critical dimensions visible; no conflict with cut list. |
| Side view | Shows 48 in run height, elevated coop, sloped roof, skids, wheels, tow tongue | 48 in run height, 96 in high side, 88 in low side, 4 ft coop depth, 10 ft total length | Buildable profile; wheel placement and ground clearance clear. |
| Exploded frame | Shows build order and major frame assemblies separated | Skids, base frame, post lines, coop floor frame, wall frames, rafters, braces | A builder can identify major assemblies without reading text. |
| Wheel/skid detail | Shows axle or spindle hardware and tow tongue | 20 in wheels, axle, collars, brackets, bolts, skid, tow ring, safety chain | Mounting method is clear enough for hardware purchase and installation. |
| Nest/roost layout | Shows interior layer configuration | Three nest boxes, roost bars, pop door, ramp, cleanout doors, ventilation | Roost/nest locations do not conflict; cleanout access remains usable. |
