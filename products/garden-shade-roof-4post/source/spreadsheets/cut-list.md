# Cut List — DIY Garden Shade Roof (Four-Post Sloped)

> Cuts are nominal for a ~8'x10' footprint with ~12" front-to-back drop.

## Posts (cut after setting)
- (2) 4x4 @ 84" — front posts (final exposed length ~7 ft)
- (2) 4x4 @ 72" — back posts (final exposed length ~6 ft)

## Beams
- (2) 2x6 @ 120" — long front and back beams (overhang both sides)

## Rafters
- (5) 2x4 @ 96" — rafters

## Knee Braces
- (4) 2x4 @ 24" — diagonal knee braces (45° cuts on each end)

## Shade Cloth Version Cuts
- (1) shade cloth: 108" x 132" (allows 6" wrap each side)

## Corrugated Panel Version Cuts
- (4) panels @ 96" — full length, overlap rib sides per panel spec.

## Notes
- Always confirm beam-and-rafter overhangs and panel overlaps before cutting.
- Use a long level and string line to set posts plumb and level.
